Overview

HELIPAD CONSULTANCY

POWERMAN provides end-to-end helipad consultancy services designed to support developers, corporations, hospitals, governments, hospitality groups, and private operators in planning and developing world-class, DGCA-compliant helipad facilities. From initial site assessment through regulatory approvals, design, and construction — we deliver practical, compliant, and future-ready solutions that support safe and efficient operations.

Services Offered
  • Site Assessment & Feasibility Studies
  • Regulatory & DGCA Approval Support
  • Rooftop Helipad Consultancy
  • Helipad Design & Infrastructure Advisory
  • LATO (Landing & Take-Off Area) Alignment
  • Firefighting, Lighting & Safety Systems
  • Operational Readiness & Safety Advisory
  • Project Management & Construction Supervision (PMC)
