POWER PLANT DECOMMISSIONING

POWERMAN provides strategic advisory services supporting power plant decommissioning, industrial asset transitions, equipment redeployment, project coordination, and asset monetisation strategies. As India's industrial landscape evolves and ageing thermal assets reach end-of-life, our advisory helps plant owners, energy companies, and industrial investors navigate the complexity of decommissioning with a clear strategy for maximising residual value.
