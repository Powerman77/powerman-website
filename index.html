Who We Are
Where Aviation Expertise
Meets Industrial Insight

POWERMAN is a New Delhi-based strategic advisory firm specialising in aviation, gas turbines, and industrial infrastructure. We bring together seasoned professionals with 20–30 years of hands-on experience drawn from DGCA, AAI Headquarters, Army Aviation, leading airlines, pilot training institutions, and global business jet operations.

We are committed to delivering solutions that are technically sound, regulatorily compliant, and commercially viable — for government organisations, aviation stakeholders, infrastructure developers, and the wider aviation fraternity.

Anil Kumaar Rai
Managing Partner, Powerman

Anil Kumaar Rai is the Managing Partner of POWERMAN, a New Delhi-based strategic advisory firm specialising in aviation, gas turbines, and industrial infrastructure. His career includes senior leadership roles at Srei Group and a JV with a firm led by a professor from MIT USA, covering infrastructure finance, mobility, and industrial sectors. Today, Anil leads POWERMAN's advisory business across India, Madagascar, and South Asia.

Helipad Design & Build Experts

Specialised expertise in helipad feasibility studies, site evaluation, helipad design, landing and take-off alignment preparation, regulatory approvals, project management consultancy (PMC), construction supervision, and end-to-end execution in compliance with DGCA and international aviation standards.

Business Jets & Helicopter Transaction Experts

Power Plant Decommissioning Experts

MRO & Technical Training Experts
